Instant micro web apps

  1. Home

Ibeyonde powers deltacatalog.com that provides online tools and hosting to create and host micro webapps. These apps specialize in a specific conversation with the customer. From being as simple as an online catalogue to more complex appointment app, these can find use in many places, for example, as a QR-code restaurant menu, as registration and check-in app for events, for visitor management or simply to send as a minified link in a text or email message.
