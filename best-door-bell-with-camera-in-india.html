Smart doorbell with camera


Introduction


The bell button press will send a Push Notification to the mobile App, either Android or iOS; usually, it hits the phone within 2 seconds. Clicking the push notification will open the live video and motion history view in the respective mobile App. Motion history lets you know who pressed the bell even if they have moved away. The live view gives you the current video feed from the bell with very low latency.

The bell can send notifications to both Android and iOS phones. The bell starts recording history on the cloud as soon as it is configured and running. The bell video feed and history can be accessed from any browser or mobile apps.

Unique Selling Points


The bell is low-cost and comes with high-quality software and hardware. It is built keeping Indian customers in mind. It comes with night-vision and a relay to drive one external doorbell. It can also ring other Ibeyonde ringers residing on the network using UDP broadcast of bell ring on the local network.

At the backend, resides a pipeline of AI/ML technologies. These are object detection, including faces and dangerous objects. Face recognition can then be enabled and configured. The bell API lets you request the visual data from the bell on demand. This enables cloud APIs that lets you query bell for detected objects, count, and scenarios at that instant. This drastically reduces the networking and storage costs.

The cloud functionality can be enabled or disabled. If disabled, the bell is only accessible from the home network, and there is no recorded history.
