Digital/Online Catalogues

  1. Home
  2. Blog
  3. Digital/Online Catalogues



You can create catalogues that not only showcase your products but also solicit feedback and contact information of the person viewing them. App Catalogues are easy to browse as there is a provision to divide and sub-divide the catalogue into sections and sub-sections. With access to menu buttons user can browse the items they are interested in. In case the user is interested in further discussion or in buying the item, that interest can be expressed through the app itself.
The account owner will have all the access to the data collected in the Dashboard. There is a provision to send messages to the user via the app itself.

The catalogues are easy to create or customise. You can pick up an existing catalogue and customize it, if editing HTML is not what you do then you can email us the pictures(or paper catalogue) and we will put one together for you. There is a option to add OTP verification of email or phone number in order to identify and communicate with your customer from dashboard once they are off the app. There is a collection of skins that will allow you to choose one that goes well with the catalogue.
