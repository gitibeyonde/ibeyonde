Travel in India

India has a variety of cultures and places to experience. There are grand sites and amazing food typical to a place. In this blog, I plan to document my experience travelling across India.

  • Pench National Park, Turia, MP

    Named after the pristine River Pence which flows through the park, Pench National Park is one of the most popular wildlife reserves in India.

  • Jammu to Hyderabad

    Jammu to Hyderabad by road, a tour of three states. Snowy peaks, grand temples, sand dunes, royal palaces, lakes and western ghats in 12 days. It was an amazing treat of vistas and food!

  • Nainital - The City of Lakes, UP

    Enveloped in a thick foil of nature, accentuated by the scent of the flowering blossoms blending miraculously in the crisp fresh air, Nainital is truly the treasure trove of Kumaon.

  • Ladakh - Land of the High Passes

    Spectacularly jagged, arid mountains enfold this magical Buddhist ex-kingdom, at north-most tip of India. Ladakh has much to teach the West regarding ecological sustainability.
